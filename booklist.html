List of Suggested Books to Begin Your DSA JOURNEY

  1. Data Structures and Algorithms Made Easy (C/C++/Java) By Narasimha Karumanchi

    One of the Best Books for detailed learning and tons of practice Questions

  2. “Introduction to Algorithms” by Thomas H. Cormen, Charles E. Leiserson, Ronald L. Rivest, and Clifford Stein

    A very practical and comprehensive overview of Algorithms

  3. Algorithms by Robert Sedgewick & Kevin Wayne

    Clear and in Depth Explanations
